Frequently Asked Questions

How is the parking fee calculated?

Parking fees are calculated based on the duration of your stay. Hourly rates apply for stays under 24 hours, daily rates for stays between 24 hours and 7 days, and weekly rates for stays of 7 days or more.

What is the grace period?

All zones include a 15-minute grace period at entry and exit. This allows you time to find your spot and load/unload your vehicle without incurring additional charges.

How do I get the online discount?

Simply book your parking online through our website before arrival. The 10% discount is automatically applied to your booking. Walk-in customers pay the standard rate.

Can I extend my parking reservation?

Yes, you can extend your parking stay. Please contact our parking office or update your reservation online before your original check-out time to avoid overstay charges.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation is available up to 2 hours before your scheduled check-in time. Cancellations made within 2 hours of check-in may be subject to charges.
